Passive HUD for motorcycles

Technology

Current helmet HUDs attempt to cram all components on the helmet. A groundbreaking optical system uses small optics on the helmet while the picture generation unit (PGU) is on the motorycle dash. A novel passive optical image stabilization ensures shake-free virtual images.
