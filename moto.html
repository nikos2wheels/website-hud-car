Passive HUD for motorcycles

Technology

Current helmet HUDs attempt to cram all components on the helmet. REBKIN developed a groundbreaking optical system that uses only small optics on the helmet while the picture generation unit (PGU) is on the motorycle dash. A novel Passive Optical Image Stabilization (POIS) system ensures steady virtual images.
