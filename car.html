Optical  solutions  for  Head-Up  Displays





Technology

Conventional HUDs are bulky and expensive devices taking up more than 4 liters of valuable dashboard space. A groundbreaking optical system for windshield-type HUDs was developed that drastically shrinks the installation volume while reducing overall cost. The system can be used with existing picture generation units (PGU). 



   Volume

The proprietary system takes up only 1/10th of the dashboard volume compared to a conventional HUD system.

   Focal point

The virtual image appears to be floating up to 6m from the driver's eyes. The driver doesn't have to shift focus from the road.

    Cost

The large focal distance naturally eliminates the ghost image. A conventional windshield can be used reducing the cost of the HUD.

Demo

The video below illustrates the benefits of the optical system on a car with a conventional windshield. Direct reflection of a LED display is on the right, collimated reflection is on the left. The ghost image is eliminated and the virtual image appears 4m outside of the windshield.
