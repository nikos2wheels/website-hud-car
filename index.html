Optical solutions for Head-Up Displays





Conventional HUD systems

Conventional HUD systems are bulky devices taking up more than 4 liters of valuable dashboard space. In addition, they require the use of wedge angle windshileds to eliminate the ghost image. This increases the cost of the system. Finally the virtual image appears to be floating about 2m from the driver, requiring the driver to change the focus from the road to the image

Volume

The proprietary system takes up only 1/10th of the dashboard volume of conventional HUD systesm.

Focal point

The virtual image appears to be floating up to 6m from the driver's eyes. The driver doesn't have to shift focus from the road.

Cost

The large focal point causes the ghost image is naturally aligned with the primary image. A regular windshield can be used reducing the cost of the HUD system.
